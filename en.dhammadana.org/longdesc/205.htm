longdesc: Double down roofs holded by fines culumns in concrete, in a garden trees well cared. On the first plan roof are protected a half-twelve guilded Buddhas statues on a socle.All are sitted in lotus style, hand in hand, going to same direction. At the back, another roof protecting a bog Buddha statue in the same position, but showing it back.
