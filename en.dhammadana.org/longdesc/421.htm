title: Yangon – Group of monks making their morning collect of food.

longdesc: A long rank of monks and novices (crossing the area from left to right) along the street, without using the pavement. Only pass a black cag with a yellow roof. Some, among novices, wearing pots. The others have a bowl. At the back, a women sweeping the pavement, and a other walks. At the end, two buildings stucked one to the other. The one on the right is in construction.
