title: Yangon – Entrance of the famous artificial cavern built for the 6th Buddhist council (in 1953 and the last in date). Today, this cavern – witch hide a magnificent and big place – is used for the big Buddhist meetings (more or less) of the country.

longdesc: At first plan, an entrance build of stones, with a ogive form. On the same axe, a High entrance going down in a massive hill with a holl of stone and concrete, imitating a cavern. In the middle of an way who joins the other of the site to the "cavern", we see two bells hanged on, on each side.
