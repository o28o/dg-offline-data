title: Tenasserim – Monastery kitchen, three monks and around ten novices live in this monastery. Every morning they go to collect their food in the village. This food is heated or prepared with wood fire, the other food brought by people who regularly come to make offerings is also prepared in this kitchen. Like everywhere else in the country, ovens, electric plates, gas cookers remain a luxury.

longdesc: Inside of a rustic kitchen, made for most with a strong table and pans. On this table and as a a oven use, bricks in the middle with choarcal keeping two pans hot. Behind this oven, a women is preparing the meal.
