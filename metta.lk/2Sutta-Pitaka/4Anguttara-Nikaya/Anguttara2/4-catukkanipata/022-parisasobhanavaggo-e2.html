CHAPTER XXII
ON ILLUMINING *

(1) Defiler and Illuminator

BRETHREN, there are these four defilers of the assembly.* What four?

Herein, brethren, a sinful brother of evil nature is a defiler of the assembly: a sinful sister of evil nature ... a sinful lay-brother of evil nature ... a sinful lay-sister of evil nature is a defiler of the assembly.

These are the four.

Brethren, there are these four who light up the assembly. What four?

Herein, brethren, there is a brother, virtuous and of goodly nature a sister a lay-brother a lay-sister, virtuous and of goodly nature. These four light up the assembly.
