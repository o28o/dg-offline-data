22. 4. 1.

(35) Màro - Death

1-3. At Sàvatthi. ... re ... The Blessed One said to venerable Ràdha who was seated on a side:

4. ßRàdha, you should dispel whatever interest for death. Ràdha, what is death? Ràdha, matter is death. Feelings, perceptions, intentions, and consciousness are death. Ràdha, you should dispel interest for them.

5. ßWherever there is death, you should dispel greed there.

6. ßWherever there is death, you should dispel interest and greed there.û

(1-3. as in 22. 4. 1.)

5. ßRàdha, you should dispel whatever interest for the nature of death.

6. ßRàdha, you should dispel greed for nature of death.

22. 4. 12.

(46) Nirodhadhamma Ý The Nature of Ceasing

1-3. Sàvatthi repeat. The Blessed One said to venerable Ràdha who was seated on a side. Ràdha, whatever is of the nature of ceasing, you should dispel interest for that. You should dispel greed for that. You should dispel interest and greed for those. Ràdha, what things are of the nature of ceasing?

4-6. ßRàdha matter is of the nature of ceasing. You should dispel interest for that. ... re ... Feelings, perceptions, intentions, and consciousness are of the nature of ceasing. You should dispel greed for those. You should dispel interest and greed for those.

7. ßRàdha, whatever is of the nature of ceasing you should dispel interest for those. You should dispel greed for those. You should dispel interest and greed for those.û
