37. 1. 1.
(1) Nibbànaü Ý Extinction

1. At one time venerable Sàriputta was living in the hamlet Nàlagà.

2. Then the wandering ascetic Jambukhàdana approached venerable Sàriputta, exchanged friendly greetings and sat on a side.

3. ßSitting on a side the wandering ascetic Jambukhàdana said to venerable Sàriputta: ßFriend Sàriputta, it is said `extinction', what is extinction?û

ßFriend, the destruction of greed, hate and delusion is extinction.û

4. ßFriend, is there a path and method for the realization of extinction?û

ßFriend, there is a path and method to realize extinction.û

5. ßFriend, what is the path and method to realize extinction?û

ßFriend, it is this same Noble Eightfold Path, for the realization of extinction, such as right view, right thoughts, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right establishment of mindfulness and concentration.û

6. ßFriend, the path and method is excellent for the realization of extinction. It is suitable that I should be diligent.û

37. 1. 3.
(3) Dhammavàdi Ý Speaking the Truth

1. At one time venerable Sàriputta was living in the hamlet Nàlagà.

2. Then the wandering ascetic Jambukhàdana approached venerable Sàriputta, exchanged friendly greetings and sat on a side.

3. ßSitting on a side the wandering ascetic Jambukhàdana said to venerable Sàriputta: ßFriend Sàriputta, who preaches the Truth in the world? Who follows the right course of action? Who fares well in the world?û

4. ßFriend, they who preach for the destruction of greed, hate and delusion speak the Truth in the world.

5. ßFriend, they who follow a course of action for the destruction of greed, hate and delusion, follow the right course of action in the world.

6. ßFriend, they who dispel greed, dispel hate and dispel delusion, dig their roots, make them palm stumps, make them not grow again, fare well in the world.'

7. ßFriend, is there a path and method to dispel greed, hate and delusion?û

ßFriend, there is a path and method to dispel greed, hate and delusion.û

8. ßFriend, what is the path and method to dispel greed, hate and delusion?û

ßFriend, it is this same Noble Eightfold Path, to dispel greed, hate and delusion, such as right view, right thoughts, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right establishment of mindfulness and right concentration.û

9. ßFriend, the path and method is excellent to dispel greed, hate and delusion. It is suitable that I should be diligent.û

37. 1. 16.
(16) Dukkaraü Ý Done With Difficulty

1. At one time venerable Sàriputta was living in the hamlet Nàlagà.

2. Then the wandering ascetic Jambukhàdana approached venerable Sàriputta, exchanged friendly greetings and sat on a side.

3. ßSitting on a side the wandering ascetic Jambukhàdana said to venerable Sàriputta: ßFriend Sàriputta, in this dispensation what is done with difficulty?û

ßFriend, going forth is difficult in this dispensation.û

4. ßFriend, what is difficult for one who has gone forth?û

ßFriend, to one gone forth to delight in the dispensation is difficult.û

5. ßûFeiend, what is the difficulty to one who delights in the dispensation?û

ßFriend, to one delighted in the dispensation living according to the Teaching is difficult.û

6. ßFriend, how long does the monk living according to the Teaching take to attain perfection?û

ßFriend, not long!û
